ABOUT US

A proficient digital communicator, specializing in multifaceted domains such as corporate branding, visual communication, social media management, photography, and videography. My wealth of experience encompasses significant internships at esteemed organizations like Newmont Africa, coupled with dedicated service as a National Service Person. During my tenure as a Graphic Designer at Citi FM/TV, I meticulously honed my expertise in media branding and the meticulous organization of visual elements for various events. My accomplishments further expanded as the Digital Media Specialist for DStv Ivory Coast, where I adeptly managed client relationships in the online sphere and optimized the social media presence of the brand. Presently, I hold the position of Marketing Communications Officer at MAC Partners Limited. My eagerness to explore diverse corporate sectors, including but not limited to mining, oil and gas, and manufacturing, is underscored by a commitment to excellence and continuous growth. Rooted in a philosophy that revolves around instigating positive change, my professional ethos extends to serving my community and country in impactful and meaningful ways. This guiding principle propels my passion for contributing to the broader landscape of corporate communications and strategic marketing.
